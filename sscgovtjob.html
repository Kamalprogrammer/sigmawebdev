ITDC Assistant Manager Recruitment 2024: Check All Eligibility Here

21/02/2024 by sscgovtjob.com

ITDC Assistant Manager Recruitment 2024: Age- Below 32 Year, Education- Diploma or Degree In Hospitality, Can apply Before 14 March.

Online applications are invited for the recruitment of the assistant manager and assistant lecturer posts. India Tourism Development Corporation Ltd. has released the latest notification for the ITDC Assistant Manager Recruitment 2024. All the candidates who want to make their career in the field of hotel management, travel departments, or hospitality can apply for this recruitment. In this article, we are going to cover all the important things related to this recruitment, like important dates, links, eligibility criteria, and others. 

All the candidates who have a 3-year diploma in hotel management and have done engineering will be eligible to apply online. The applicant’s age must be below 32 years old to apply for any post-givan in this notification. The final selection for this recruitment will be made after clearing all the eligibility requirements and after conducting an interview. After selection, all the candidates will receive six LPA packages annually. All the applicants who match all the eligibility criteria can apply before March 14, 2024. ITDC Assistant Manager Recruitment 2024 Age Criteria

To apply for these posts, that notification is released in that those candidates who are applying for Assistant Manager, Chef, or Assistant Lecturer posts must be under 30 years old. 

ITDC Assistant Manager Recruitment 2024 Application Fee

Candidates are required to pay a non-refundable application fee of Rs. 500 (five hundred only) (excluding applicable convenience fees and taxes). Further, candidates belonging to the SC, ST, and PwD categories are exempt from paying the application fee.

ITDC Assistant Manager Recruitment 2024 Important Documents

  • Birth Certificate/Matriculation Certificate in proof of Date of Birth.
  • Academic, Technical & Professional Degrees/Certificates.
  • Experience certificates.
  • Caste/Disability Certificate.
  • Present employment proof, if applicable.

ITDC Assistant Manager Recruitment Selection Process

Final selection will be made for these vacancies on the basis of interview and complete eligibilities. Those candidate who will meet all the eligibilities they will be shortlisted.

ITDC Assistant Manager How to Apply

  • Visit the official portal of BEL India @https://itdc.co.in/careers/
  • Click on the career option given at the top of the website.
  • Will get recruitment Section if Not Click Here.
  • After this click on to Fill Online Application Form.
  • Sign if you already registered or register yourself if you are applying for the first time.
  • Then Complete details asked in the Application form.
  • Download and hard copy the application form.
